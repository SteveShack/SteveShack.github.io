A Squirrely Story

A Squirrely Story is a game I made in collaboration with 14 others in my Game Studio 2 Class. My role in the project was to act as Product Owner and General Designer as I oversaw all aspects such as Level Design, Narrative, and the overall vision of the game.

"This game proved that I absolutely love making games with others!"

Working on this game was truly an experience as it originated as a penguin throwing magic cards to a squirrel throwing acorns!

Everyone that worked on this project did an absolute amazing job, and I worked hours on end to ensure that our game was ready for testing and finalized when it came to project submission.
